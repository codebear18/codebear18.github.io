Evil Dog

My dog Gracie is quite evil. I mean, she can be sweet at
some times but most of the time, she is just plain evil.
I think that she may just be a cat. She lays around all
day long, and completely ignores most people, unless food
is involved. She is a beagle, but she is the smallest
beagle I have ever seen. I honestly think she hates me if I
don't have food for her at all times.

She always wants to eat and will not stop eating as long
as there is food in front of her. Because of this, she may
have gotten a little... over weight. However, she has shed a
few extra pounds on her diet. She is 10 years old, so she
is the grandma of my dog family. Even Lucy, who is about
three times her size, is scared of her. She is pretty
feisty for how small she is, but she is still super
sweet and lovable at times.

New Puppy

I just got a new puppy! Her name is Rosie, and she is the sweetest puppy ever.
She loves to give hugs, and will not hesitate in diving onto your lap to be pet.
She is Lucy's new best friend and they love to play together. Lucy is very excited
to have another puppy around.

Rosie is very playful. She loves to play with all of her toys, and she
is not big on sharing. She is learning quickly, she already knows sit, stay, and
we're still working on getting her off the sofa. She is an extremely happy little
addition to my family of pets and I know we are all very happy to have her.
